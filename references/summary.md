| **Authors**                                                                                                                               | **Title**                                                                                                     | **Summary (5–6 words)**                        | **Limitations**                                                                                                    |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Mahsa Samkhaniani, Afshin Khoshand, Soran Ezati (2025)                                                                                    | _Deep Learning-Based Hyperspectral Oil Spill Detection for Marine Pollution Monitoring in the Gulf of Mexico_ | Hyperspectral + DL improves oil detection.     | • Class imbalance in dataset<br>• Focus only on hyperspectral                                                      |
| Konstantinos Gkountakos, Maria Melitou, Konstantinos Ioannidis, Konstantinos Demestichas, Stefanos Vrochidis, Ioannis Kompatsiaris (2025) | _LADOS: Aerial Imagery Dataset for Oil Spill Detection_                                                       | UAV RGB dataset with six classes.              | • Only RGB data<br>• Possible dataset bias                                                                         |
| Jianting Shi, Tianyu Jiao, Daniel P. Ames, Zhijun Li (2025)                                                                               | _Improved Lightweight Marine Oil Spill Detection Algorithm of YOLOv8_                                         | Lightweight YOLOv8 for faster SAR detection.   | • SAR only<br>• Custom dataset limits generalization                                                               |
| Yu Zhang, Jian Xing, Weida Chen, Haitao Wang, Bingyu Shi, Yang Song, Xiaoou Huang, Zihan Jiang (2025)                                     | _YOLOv11 for UAV Multispectral Oil Spill Detection_                                                           | UAV multispectral YOLOv11 detects spills.      | • Small dataset<br>• High false detections in turbid water                                                         |
| Jonggu Kang, Chansu Yang, Jonghyuk Yi, Yangwon Lee (2024)                                                                                 | _Oil Spill Detection from PlanetScope Images_                                                                 | CNN vs Transformer on optical satellite data.  | • Optical-only, weather dependent<br>• Limited real-time use                                                       |
| Zhen Sun, Qingshu Yang, Nanyang Yan, Siyu Chen, Jianhang Zhu, Jun Zhao, Shaojie Sun (2024)                                                | _Utilizing Deep Learning Algorithms for Automated Oil Spill Detection in Medium Resolution Optical Imagery_   | CNNs with attention boost optical detection.   | • Optical-only, weather/cloud limitations<br>• Needs large labeled datasets<br>• False positives without sun glint |
| Jiahao Zhang, Pengju Yang, Xincheng Ren (2024)                                                                                            | _Detection of Oil Spill in SAR Image Using an Improved DeepLabV3+_                                            | DeepLabv3+ segments oil in SAR.                | • SAR look-alikes cause misclassification<br>• Generalization across regions uncertain                             |
| Merv Fingas (2021)                                                                                                                        | _Visual Appearance of Oil on the Sea_                                                                         | Oil appearance indicates thickness, condition. | • Reliable only for thin slicks<br>• Influenced by light, weather, oil type                                        |
| Marios Krestenitis, Georgios Orfanidis, Konstantinos Ioannidis, Konstantinos Avgerinakis, Stefanos Vrochidis, Ioannis Kompatsiaris (2019) | _Oil Spill Identification from Satellite Images Using Deep Neural Networks_                                   | SAR + DCNN segmentation for oil detection.     | • Class imbalance<br>• Confusion between oil spills and look-alikes<br>• Limited ship detection accuracy           |
| Filippo Bianchi, Martine M. Espeseth, Njal Borch (2020)                                                                                   | _Large-Scale Detection and Categorization of Oil Spills from SAR_                                             | CNN on Sentinel-1 SAR, large dataset.          | • Costly labeling<br>• SAR struggles with look-alikes                                                              |
| Fabio Del Frate, Andrea Petrocchi, Juerg Lichtenegger, Gianna Calabresi (2000)                                                            | _Neural Networks for Oil Spill Detection Using ERS-SAR Data_                                                  | Early NN classifies oil vs look-alike.         | • Small dataset<br>• Manual preprocessing needed<br>• Limited generalization                                       |
